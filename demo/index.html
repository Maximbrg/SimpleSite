Pudge

Although he is often asked, Pudge refuses to answer any questions about his family, and gets quite irate at curious strangers.
In his youth, he served in the employ of King Brandalon as his royal butcher.
This was the beginning of Pudge's career, which he considered the best years of his life.
The King's reign was particularly bloody, and Pudge is one of the few who are still alive to remember the carnage.
At some point, he also spent some time on a farm.
Seeking meat, Pudge journeyed to the Fields of Endless Carnage, where he loomed over the dead, cutting up and devouring their flesh.
For reasons unknown, he left the Fields before the Rumusque Clerics made a pilgrimage there to purge the land.
